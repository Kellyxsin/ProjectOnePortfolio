About

Kelly loves being a designer/artist because she gets to create and show her work as something to fulfill her life. She wants to learn more because she is in the beginning stages of what it is to be in the creative field of design. Kelly still feels that she is in the beginning stages of her major in design but is actually in the middle. Everything is going so fast for her and she worries a lot. She does not know what truly inspires her because she has a hard time figuring out what really likes. She thinks too much of the details but has some goals. Her goals so far are to graduate college and to find a job that has something to do with graphic design. Kelly is from San Francisco and has never left. Also, she came from a humble background and is trying her best to be herself and never become naive. This has made her who she is today because of her family and friends that keep her grounded. They always tell her to be respectful towards others and to act bold and tough. Kelly has tried to act bold and tough like her family but she has social anxiety. After Kelly graduates, she wants to find a job that is related to her creative field. Before that she needs connections and builds a community of designers who are on the same boat as her. Kelly feels that things should slow down because of some anxiety that goes into this type of major. It is tough because after college, she would become indecisive but figure out things at the same time. Kelly is trying to be more passionate about design because it is interesting and she wants to know more in a step by step process.
